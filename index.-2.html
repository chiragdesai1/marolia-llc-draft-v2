About Subway

Subway is one of the world's largest restaurant chains, known for its made-to-order sandwiches, salads, and wraps. Each restaurant offers:

  • Fresh-baked bread throughout the day
  • Wide variety of fresh vegetables
  • Multiple protein options including turkey, ham, chicken, and plant-based alternatives
  • Signature sauces and seasonings
  • Breakfast menu items
  • Catering services
